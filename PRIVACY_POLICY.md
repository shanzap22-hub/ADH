# ADH Connect - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: February 6, 2026

## Introduction

ADH Connect ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following personal information:
- **Email address** - For account creation and authentication
- **Name** - For your profile and personalization
- **Profile photo** - Optional, for profile customization
- **Phone number** - Optional, for WhatsApp notifications and booking confirmations

### Usage Data
- **Course progress** - To track your learning journey
- **Course enrollments** - To manage your purchased courses
- **Booking information** - For live session scheduling
- **Chat messages** - For AI chat and community features
- **Device information** - Device type, OS version (for app functionality)

### Analytics Data
- **App usage patterns** - Via Firebase Analytics (aggregated only)
- **Crash reports** - Via Firebase Crashlytics (for debugging)
- **Push notification tokens** - For OneSignal push notifications

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain our LMS services
- Authenticate your account securely
- Track your course progress
- Send course notifications and updates
- Provide AI-powered chat assistance
- Schedule and manage live sessions
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage & Security

### Secure Storage
- **Authentication tokens** - Stored using Android Keystore encryption
- **User credentials** - Encrypted and stored securely on your device
- **Course data** - Stored on Supabase (hosted database)

### Security Measures
- HTTPS-only connections (no HTTP)
- Code obfuscation (ProGuard)
- Secure WebView configuration
- No backup of sensitive data
- Regular security audits

## Data Sharing

### We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your personal data
- ❌ Share data with advertisers
- ❌ Track you across other apps

### We DO Share Data With:
- **Firebase** (Google) - Analytics, crash reporting, authentication
- **Supabase** - Database hosting for course and user data
- **OneSignal** - Push notifications
- **Resend** - Email notifications

All third-party services comply with GDPR and data protection regulations.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** - View all data we have about you
- **Export** - Download your data in a portable format
- **Delete** - Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Opt-out** - Disable push notifications, analytics, or marketing emails

### How to Exercise Your Rights:
1. Email us at: **privacy@adh.today**
2. Or use the in-app settings to manage your preferences

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA).

## Cookies & Tracking

We use:
- **Essential cookies** - For authentication and app functionality
- **Analytics cookies** - Firebase Analytics (aggregated data only)
- **Local storage** - For offline caching and app performance

You can disable analytics in the app settings.

## Data Retention

We retain your data:
- **Active accounts** - Until you delete your account
- **Deleted accounts** - Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Course progress** - Kept indefinitely unless you request deletion
- **Analytics data** - Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Sending an email notification
- Showing an in-app notification

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **Google Play Store Data Safety requirements**
- **Android 14/15 privacy requirements**
